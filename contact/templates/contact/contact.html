Commonly Asked Questions:

If you are not able to find the answer below, feel free to contact us!
We answer within 1-2 business days.

How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary depending on your location and the specific items ordered. Typically, orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and standard shipping within the continental US takes 3-5 business days.
What if my plant arrives damaged?
We take great care in packaging our plants to ensure they arrive in optimal condition. In the rare event that your plant arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team with a photo of the plant within 24 hours of delivery, and we'll be happy to assist you with a replacement or refund.
Do you offer gift wrapping services?
Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for a small additional fee. Simply select the gift wrapping option at checkout, and we'll make sure your order is beautifully wrapped and ready to be presented.
