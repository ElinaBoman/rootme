How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary depending on your location and the specific items ordered. Typically, orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and standard shipping within the continental US takes 3-5 business days.
What if my plant arrives damaged?
We take great care in packaging our plants to ensure they arrive in optimal condition. In the rare event that your plant arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team with a photo of the plant within 24 hours of delivery, and we'll be happy to assist you with a replacement or refund.
Do you offer gift wrapping services?
Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for a small additional fee. Simply select the gift wrapping option at checkout, and we'll make sure your order is beautifully wrapped and ready to be presented.
What factors influence the pricing of cuttings?
The pricing of cuttings is influenced by a variety of factors that collectively determine the value of the plant material. Firstly, rarity plays a significant role; cuttings from rare or exotic plant species are often priced higher due to their limited availability in the market. Additionally, the demand for specific plants can heavily impact their pricing; popular or trendy plants may command higher prices due to increased demand from enthusiasts and collectors.
Which types of pests can be controlled using beneficial insects?
Beneficial insects can be used to control a wide range of pests in the garden and crops. Examples of pests that can be controlled using beneficial insects include aphids, thrips, spider mites, leafhoppers, caterpillars, and whiteflies. If you're unsure about which beneficial insects you need for your specific situation, feel free to contact Root|Me for personalized advice and recommendations.
